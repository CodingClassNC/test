Assault Rifle

The Assault Rifle is a versatile automatic weapon suitable for medium to long-range combat. It has decent damage and accuracy, making it a popular choice among players.

Pump Shotgun

The Pump Shotgun is a powerful close-range weapon that can deal massive damage with a single shot. It's deadly in tight spaces and provides an advantage in close-quarter battles.

Bolt Action Sniper

The Bolt Action Sniper is a high-powered rifle capable of dealing massive damage to opponents from a long distance. It's perfect for picking off enemies from afar.

Rocket Launcher

The Rocket Launcher is a devastating explosive weapon that can deal massive damage to structures and opponents in a large area. It's great for breaking defenses and causing chaos in battles.

Submachine Gun

The Submachine Gun is a rapid-firing weapon suitable for close to medium-range combat. It excels in fast-paced fights and can quickly shred opponents at close quarters.

Grenade Launcher

The Grenade Launcher fires explosive projectiles that bounce and explode on impact. It's an excellent tool for destroying enemy structures and forcing opponents out of cover.
