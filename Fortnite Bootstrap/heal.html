Small Shields

Small Shield Potion is an Uncommon Healing Item in Battle Royale. The Small Shield Potion takes 2 seconds to consume, and upon consumption grants 25 Shield to a maximum of 50.

Big Pot

Big Pot is an Epic Healing Item in Battle Royale. It takes 5 seconds to consume, and upon consumption grants 100 Shield to a maximum of 100. Big Pot is found in Chests, Supply Drops, and Loot Carriers.

Chug Jug

Chug Jug is a Legendary Healing Item in Battle Royale. It takes 15 seconds to consume, and upon consumption grants full health and full shield. The Chug Jug is found in Supply Drops and Loot Carriers.

Bandages

Bandages are a Common Healing Item in Battle Royale. They heal the player for 15 Health per use, up to a maximum of 75. Bandages can be found in floor loot and chests.

Med Kit

Med Kit is a Rare Healing Item in Battle Royale. It takes 10 seconds to consume, and upon consumption, it heals the player to full health. Med Kits are found in floor loot and chests.

Slurp Juice

Slurp Juice is an Epic Healing Item in Battle Royale. It grants 75 Health and/or Shield, depending on the player's current health. If the player's health is already full, it grants 75 Shield. Slurp Juice can be found in floor loot, chests, and supply drops.
